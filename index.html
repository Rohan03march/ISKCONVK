About Vaikunta Hill

The Rajadhiraja Govinda Temple at Vaikunta Hill, Vasanthapura was inaugurated by Honourable President of India, Sri Ram Nath Kovind, on 14 June, 2022 during the Lokarpana ceremony.

Srila Prabhupada desired to build a grand temple in Bangalore and this Rajadhiraja Govinda Temple is a humble offering to Srila Prabhupada to commemorate his 125th birth anniversary.

The architecture of this traditional, stone-carved temple resembles that of Tirumala and the Deity of Sri Rajadhiraja Govinda is also a replica of Lord Venkateshwara.

How To Reach?

Route to vaikuntha hill

There is Metro train connectivity (Green-line) and are many city buses available from Majestic (Bus stand) K R Market and Banashankari to reach Vaikuntha Hill. You have to alight at the stop – Doddakallasandra (Kanakapura Road) and it takes about 10 minutes to reach the temple by road and around 20 minutes by walk.
